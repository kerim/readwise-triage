# Reader Persona

Describe your reading interests, research areas, and priorities here. This file is sent to Claude Haiku during pitch generation to personalize the why-read and why-skip analysis.

## Example

I'm a software engineer interested in distributed systems, programming language design, and the history of computing. I read widely on climate policy and urban planning. I'm currently researching WebAssembly runtimes for a side project.

When triaging, I prioritize:
- Technical deep-dives over news coverage
- Primary sources over commentary
- Anything related to my current research topics

I tend to skip:
- Listicles and "10 things" articles
- Vendor marketing disguised as blog posts
- Topics I've already read extensively on (e.g., blockchain)
